Algorithm Efficiency

Each algorithms have their own strength and weakness. Metaheuristic algorithms such as hill climbing, simulated annealing, simulated annealing with reheat, genetic algorithm, hybridised genetic algorithm and simulated annealing and ant colony optimisation are tested on the datasets from TSPLIB. Nearest neighbour algorithm is excluded from the testing because it is a pure greedy algorithm instead of metaheristic algorithm.

The testing are conducted with the following conditions:

  1. the parameters and settings of algorithms are same for every dataset
  2. each algorithms would have 30 trials for every dataset
